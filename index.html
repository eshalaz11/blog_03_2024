How AI can automate AI research and development

https://www.rand.org/pubs/commentary/2024/10/how-ai-can-automate-ai-research-and-development.html October 24 2024

The article explains that companies now use AI to help design and improve new AI technology faster, with less human involvement. This automation speeds up research by having AI programs perform tasks that normally need a lot of human work. However, it raises concerns about safety and control, as the technology develops rapidly. The main tech discussed is AI systems that automate parts of AI research and coding

Is AI Threatening Health Care Jobs? Or Just Changing Them?

https://www.rand.org/pubs/commentary/2024/09/is-ai-threatening-health-care-jobs-or-just-changing.html September 16 2024

The article explains that AI in healthcare is mainly helping with administrative tasks, reducing workloads, and supporting staff, rather than taking over jobs. Tasks like documentation and appointment management benefit from AI, saving time and helping to ease worker burnout
